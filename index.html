HELLO WORLD

This is the part where most people brag about what they have done and who they have worked with. That’s not who I am. So here we go… I am Mutiny Audio. This name does not necessarily represent a person nor is it a pseudonym to mask who I am in the “real world”. Mutiny Audio is the externalization of my thoughts and emotions in the form of music & art. In modern society we have the ability to share everything from humor to misery in many different ways. These creations, along with many other things scattered across cyberspace will be here long after I am gone. I hope you enjoy what I have to share.
